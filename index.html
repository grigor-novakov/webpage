About Me

My area of expertise ranges from Enterprise Software to Professional Services, delivering high impact and high value programs of work for major Australian Government and private organisations.

Portfolio

Australia's Digital Birth Certificate

I was part of the Leadership Team for the program of work that delivered the MVP of Australia's first Digital Bird Certificate. Key responsibilities included collaboration with NSW Department of Customer Service ensuring alignment between the Federal and State authorities. Some critical tasks involved managing multiple squads working on the initial prototype, delivery and testing strategy, identity management and customer onboarding experience.

QLD Digital Driving License

I led a multi-squad cross functional team that delivered the microservices platform (MEDA) that enables the QLD Digital License App and ensures its scalability, stability and reliability, as well as delivering the actual microservices to extract data from the Department of Transport and Main Roads mainframe system and feed into the License App.

Buy.NSW

I led the re-build and re-platforming of the official NSW Government tender platform, Buy.NSW. Managed a multi-squad cross functional team working closely with NSW Procurement and Department of Customer Service to scope, architecture and design the new procurement experience, and deliver the program of work from MVP through to complete solution over four project stages. This project won 2nd place in the internal NSW Digital Government Awards scoring closely behind the NSW Digital Driving License.
